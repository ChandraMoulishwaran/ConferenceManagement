Welcome to Conference Management System

A conference system that allows attendees to register for a conference and submit their paper's title and abstract online. The system provides a platform for organizers to collect and review submissions efficiently and manage attendee registrations. This system is helpful for academic or research-based conferences where paper submissions are an essential part of the conference. By providing an online registration and submission process, the system streamlines the conference organization process and simplifies communication between organizers and attendees.
